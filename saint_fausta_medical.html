We Are a PhilHealth
YAKAP-Accredited Clinic

Saint Fausta Medical is proud to be an accredited provider under PhilHealth's YAKAP (Yaman ng Kalusugan Program) — a government program that gives every PhilHealth member access to free primary care, laboratory tests, medicines, and cancer screening at no out-of-pocket cost.

Laboratory & Imaging

Diagnostics

Our laboratory is equipped with modern analyzers and staffed by licensed medical technologists, ensuring accurate and timely results for all tests.

We process specimens on-site for most routine tests, with results typically available same-day or within 24 hours.

  • Laboratory Tests (CBC, Urinalysis, Blood Chemistry, Fecalysis & more)
  • Ultrasound (Abdominal, OB, Soft Tissue)
  • X-Ray (Digital Chest & Bone Imaging)
  • ECG / 12-Lead EKG
  • 2-D Echocardiography (2-D Echo)
  • Papsmear
  • Drug Testing (DOH-Accredited)
  • And other diagnostic procedures
💡 Fasting is required for some blood chemistry tests. Please call ahead to confirm preparation requirements for your specific test.
Full-Service Pharmacy

Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy stocks a comprehensive range of branded and generic medications, vaccines, vitamins, and medical supplies — all dispensed by registered pharmacists.

We honor all government-mandated Senior Citizen and PWD discounts with valid IDs. Prescription medicines are dispensed strictly following DOH regulations.

  • Maintenance medications (hypertension, diabetes, cholesterol)
  • Prescription & OTC medications
  • Flu, Pneumonia & other vaccines
  • Vitamins, minerals & supplements
  • Wound care & first aid supplies
  • Blood pressure monitors & glucometers
  • Senior Citizen & PWD discounts honored
💡 Need to check availability of a specific medicine? Call us before visiting to save time — our pharmacist will confirm stock on the spot.
Consultations & Exams

Check-ups

Our licensed physicians provide general health consultations, annual physical exams, and all required medical certificates for employment, school, and legal purposes.

Walk-ins are welcome for most check-up services. For occupational health packages and group APEs, we recommend scheduling in advance.

  • Annual Physical Exam (APE)
  • Pre-employment Medical Exam
  • General Health Consultation
  • Medical Certificate (SLMC / Clearance)
  • School & University Health Clearance
  • Fitness-to-Work Certification
  • Barangay / LGU Health Certificate
💡 Bring a valid government-issued ID for all medical certificate requests. Results and certificates are typically released on the same day.
