Laboratory & Imaging

Diagnostics

Our laboratory is equipped with modern analyzers and staffed by licensed medical technologists, ensuring accurate and timely results for all tests.

We process specimens on-site for most routine tests, with results typically available same-day or within 24 hours.

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Blood Chemistry (FBS, Lipid Profile, Creatinine, Uric Acid, SGPT/SGOT)
  • Urinalysis & Fecalysis
  • Thyroid Function Tests (TSH, FT3, FT4)
  • HbA1c & Blood Glucose Monitoring
  • Chest X-Ray (Digital)
  • Abdominal & OB Ultrasound
  • ECG / 12-Lead EKG

Full-Service Pharmacy

Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy stocks a comprehensive range of branded and generic medications, vaccines, vitamins, and medical supplies — all dispensed by registered pharmacists.

We honor all government-mandated Senior Citizen and PWD discounts with valid IDs. Prescription medicines are dispensed strictly following DOH regulations.

  • Maintenance medications (hypertension, diabetes, cholesterol)
  • Prescription & OTC medications
  • Flu, Pneumonia & other vaccines
  • Vitamins, minerals & supplements
  • Wound care & first aid supplies
  • Blood pressure monitors & glucometers
  • Senior Citizen & PWD discounts honored

Who We Are

Built for the Antipolo Community

Saint Fausta Medical Diagnostic and Pharmacy Co. was established to bring reliable, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to the residents of Antipolo, Rizal.

We operate as a fully integrated medical facility — combining laboratory diagnostics, imaging, pharmacy services, and medical consultations under one roof — so that every patient can access comprehensive care without going elsewhere.

Our team consists of licensed physicians, medical technologists, radiologic technologists, and registered pharmacists committed to your health every step of the way.
